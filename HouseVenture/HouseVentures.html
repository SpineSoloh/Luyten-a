Our Services

Our specially designed services, such as resume writing, LinkedIn profile optimization, and interview coaching, will communicate your genuine value proposition, establish your expertise, and meet the employer's particular needs - maximizing your chance to find a job you'll enjoy.

Resume/cover letter Writing

Communicate your Value to Employers: You need a branded marketing document that positions you for the role whether you're looking for a new job, a promotion, or a career change. At House Ventures Staffing Consultant, we highlight your most pertinent experience, accomplishments that are focused on results, and important skills to get beyond applicant tracking systems (ATS) and show potential employers the depth and scope of your responsibilities.

LinkedIn optimization

Enhance your brand: The largest professional network and applicant sourcing platform in the world is LinkedIn. Let's say that your goal on LinkedIn is to attract hiring managers or recruiters, promote business opportunities, or develop a strong network. In such situation, you require an authentic online presence that uses storytelling to highlight your personality, values, and domain knowledge.

Interview coaching

Have the assurance to succeed in your upcoming job interview. Do you have an interview coming up? Would you like the self-assurance to persuade the hiring manager that you are the ideal person for the position? What if you could prepare for the actual interview, learn the best interview questions to ask and how to respond to them, identify your shortcomings and get feedback on them before you meet the employer? Your interviewing abilities will make or break your chances of getting a job.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We provide help with job applications. They include career counselling, cover letter and resume writing, LinkedIn profile updating, and pre-interview preparation.

Why should I entrust my career to House Ventures Staffing Consultant?

We have the knowledge to assist you in developing a plan that is unique to you and your objectives.

What is the writing process?

You will first complete a questionnaire and have a strategy call with a writer who is an authority in your field.

How long is your turnaround time?

You will get the first draft within 2-4 days of completing the consultation call with your dedicated writer. You may expedite the delivery time at an additional cost.

Do you write international resume?

Yes, we do. We will tailor your resume to the expectations and standards of your country, including the generally accepted format and principle writing guidelines.
