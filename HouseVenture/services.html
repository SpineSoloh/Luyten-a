Our Services

Services We Offer

Our specially designed services, such as resume writing, LinkedIn profile optimization, and interview coaching, will communicate your genuine value proposition, establish your expertise, and meet the employer's particular needs - maximizing your chance to find a job you'll enjoy.

Resume Writing

Communicate your Value to Employers: You need a branded marketing document that positions you for the role whether you're looking for a new job, a promotion, or a career change. At House Ventures Staffing Consultant, we highlight your most pertinent experience, accomplishments that are focused on results, and important skills to get beyond applicant tracking systems (ATS) and show potential employers the depth and scope of your responsibilities.

Your resume serves as a snapshot of your prior achievements that are pertinent to the position you are seeking as well as a predictor of future success to potential employers. We'll extol your virtues and mould them to the desired position.

Be Different from the Crowd: By creating strategic resumes that outperform the competition, we assist veterans, transitioning service members, national and local government employees, senior leaders and executives, academic and medical professionals, supervisors and mid-level managers, entry-level workers, students, and recent graduates in getting off to a strong start.
Get the job: We'll work with you to develop a focused CV/resume that connects your experience to the civil service or federal job posting you're applying for. Then, in order to make your application more persuasively speak to the needs of potential employers and get you forwarded to the Hiring Manager, we will edit your resume to ensure that it complies with industry standards and HR screening criteria.

LinkedIn optimization

Enhance your brand: The largest professional network and applicant sourcing platform in the world is LinkedIn. Let's say that your goal on LinkedIn is to attract hiring managers or recruiters, promote business opportunities, or develop a strong network. In such situation, you require an authentic online presence that uses storytelling to highlight your personality, values, and domain knowledge.
Get the Most Exposure: More than 90% of recruiters search LinkedIn for qualified prospects. To help you outrank the competition in LinkedIn searches and stand out to potential employers, House Ventures Staffing Consultant will optimize your LinkedIn profile using sector-specific keywords and desirable abilities.
Research: For the purpose of conducting keyword research for your job hunt, House Ventures Staffing Consultant will look into your target market, business, and roles. Then, we'll compile the facts you want to convey, identify your strongest points, learn more about your professional background, and compile data about your education to create a persuasive campaign that promotes your value proposition.
Focused Headline: A 120-character, keyword-driven, targeted headline tailored to your industry sector, business, and position goals will be created by House Ventures Staffing Consultant. Your targeted title will assist you in grabbing the attention of the right audience and making an impression, frequently resulting in increased recruiter engagement and lead turnover.
Profile Rewrite: A 2000-character professional summary will be written by a staffing consultant from House Ventures that highlights your particular professional history, harmonizes it with your resume, and incorporates the most in-demand keywords and targeted hashtags. Your marketing message will remain laser-focused on your objective, with a call to action at the end (CTA)..

Interview coaching

Do you have an interview coming up? Would you like the self-assurance to persuade the hiring manager that you are the ideal person for the position? What if you could prepare for the actual interview, learn the best interview questions to ask and how to respond to them, identify your shortcomings and get feedback on them before you meet the employer? Your interviewing abilities will make or break your chances of getting a job.

In order for you to succeed in your next interview, House Ventures Staffing Consultant will assist you in overcoming your interview anxieties, honing your elevator pitch, and answering even the trickiest interview questions. You will leave the interview prepared, confident, and able to sell your talents and credentials to any challenging interviewer.

90% of the interview coaching questions will, without a doubt, be asked during the actual job interview. You should take advantage of the opportunity to practice and be prepared with the answers.

Resume Revamp

Executive Resume; senior managers, department heads, directors, VPs, and C-level executives should use this CV. We write powerful statements that emphasize your leadership responsibility, fundamental judgment, and accomplishments that are motivated by values. We also assist you win 6-figure pay opportunities by showcasing your overall value proposition through a combination of your talents, charisma, and knowledge.
Professional Resume: This resume is intended for entry-level workers and mid-level managers who are employed in corporate positions with businesses in the private sector or NGOs. We measure your greatest successes and provide a summary of your technical and soft talents that are relevant to the job's stated responsibilities. We concentrate on effectively articulating your value and showcasing your personal brand without overusing industry jargon.
Academic/Medical Resume: For professionals looking for academic, medical, scientific, or research roles, an academic/medical CV is their main job-search tool. We customize it with the least amount of jargon possible to highlight your qualifications and contributions to your particular field of study or profession, including your academic successes, research interests and their implications, specialized skills, publications, grants and fellowships, professional associations, licenses, and awards.
Graduate Resume: Graduate resumes are made specifically for fresh graduates or students who have no prior work experience. We put a lot of emphasis on showcasing your academic accomplishments, hard talents, soft skills, and internship experience. Along with listing your accomplishments and academic honours, we'll also describe any leadership positions you've held in extracurricular activities, campus organizations, and teams.
Federal Resume (USAJOBS): Use this resume while applying for professional, technical, administrative, and clerical positions with the Federal Government. We use the job description's keywords to find candidates that satisfy the hiring manager's requirements while also meeting the Executive Core Qualifications (ECQs). We make sure that the name, phone number, 4-digit job series number, pay plan (GS/FWS), pay grade, and number of hours worked each week are all disclosed.
Military Resume: This resume is intended military people making the transition from active duty to civilian employment. We describe your professional background and military accomplishments in plain language, as well as how your skill set relates to the job posting. We highlight the positive parts of your deployment, such as the military occupation specialization, the length of service, the awards received, the most recent rank and pay grade, security clearances, education, and training.
